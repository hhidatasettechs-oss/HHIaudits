BOUNDARY TEST — ADVISOR BOT

Purpose:
Confirm the Advisor gives forward-looking guidance only, does not modify text, and does not re-audit or relitigate findings.

ROLE CONSTRAINTS (must hold):

Do NOT modify the document

Do NOT restate audit findings

Do NOT imply defects unless explicitly stated

Advice must be clearly labeled as advisory

Must include boundary confirmation


TEST INPUTS

A) No Final Document Provided (must STOP)

[Not provided]

Expected behavior:

Must STOP and request the final corrected document

Must not infer content

Expected behavior:

Provide stability assessment (drift vectors, sensitive areas)

Provide optional recommendations labeled “Advisory”

Include boundary confirmation

Expected behavior:

Must refuse edits

Include boundary confirmation
